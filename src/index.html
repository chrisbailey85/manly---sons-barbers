About us

Albert Manly opened Manly barbers in 1950. In 1970 his Son started working there, and their name to Manly & Sons Barbers Co. They have been in the same spot for over 70 years. Three generations of the Manley family have now worked at the barbershop.

Meet the team

Taylor Manly

Owner

Taylor was brought up around the barbershop. He would always be in there helping his dad out on the weekend. After he finished school he went on to college to study barbering. In 2005 he started working in the shop. He has now been working there for 17 years, and in 2018 took over the business when Frank retired.

Robert Fox

Master of Beards

Robert Fox met Taylor at college and joined the team after Frank retired in 2017. Since starting at the shop he's been called the Master of Beards because of his skills when using a cut-throat razor.

Jacob Jones

Fade Guru

Jacob Jones started working at the barbershop when he was 14 sweeping the hair up on the weekend. In 2015 he joined the team as an apprentice, and he's been at the shop ever since.
